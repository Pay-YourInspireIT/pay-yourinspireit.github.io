Common Wallet Questions

Find quick answers to frequently asked questions about the Your Inspire IT Wallet.

Once logged in, navigate to the 'Withdraw' or 'Wallet' section. Select your preferred payment method (e.g., Bkash, Nagad), enter the amount (meeting the minimum threshold), and confirm. Follow any necessary verification steps.

The minimum withdrawal amount can vary depending on the chosen payment method. The specific minimum for each option (like Bkash, Nagad, Rocket) is clearly displayed when you start the withdrawal process.

Withdrawals are typically processed within 3-7 business days after your request is reviewed and approved. Processing times might slightly vary based on the payment provider.

Your Inspire IT aims to keep fees low. However, some payment methods (like mobile banking or international transfers) might have small processing fees charged by the provider themselves. Any applicable fees from our side or the provider will be clearly shown before you confirm the withdrawal.
